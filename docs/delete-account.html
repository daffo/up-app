Delete Your Account

UP App by daffo

You can delete your UP account and all associated data directly from the app.

Steps to delete your account

  1. Open the UP app on your device
  2. Log in to your account if you aren't already
  3. Tap your profile icon and go to My Account
  4. Scroll down and tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm the deletion when prompted

What data is deleted

Permanently deleted immediately:

  • Your user profile and display name
  • Your logged sends and ratings
  • Your comments on routes
  • Routes you created
  • Your authentication credentials

Not affected:

  • Wall photos and detected holds (shared resources, not tied to individual accounts)

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. If you have trouble deleting your account, contact us at dafffo@gmail.com.
